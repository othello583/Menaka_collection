Weaving Tradition

From the heart of Manipur, Menaka Collection brings you the finest handwoven textiles — each piece a testament to generations of craftsmanship, culture, and pride.

What began as a passion for preserving authentic Manipuri weaving traditions has grown into a thriving business trusted by thousands of customers across the region and beyond.
